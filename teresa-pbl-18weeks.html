🦎 Teresa's 18-Week PBL Plan

My Community & Our Changing World | For Grade 3-4 (Ages 8-9)

Core Question: How can I learn, create, and help make my community and world better?
Language: English-focused | Duration: 18 weeks, 3-4 sessions/week, 20-30 min/session

🌍 Weeks 1-4: Community & City Life

Week 1-2: My Neighborhood
  • 📖 Read: "My Neighborhood" English picture books
  • 🔍 Research: Map of Beijing, different places in community
  • ✏️ Write: Description of favorite places
  • 🎨 Create: Draw your neighborhood map
  • 🗣️ Speak: Present to family
Week 3-4: Community Helpers
  • 📖 Read: Stories about doctors, teachers, firefighters
  • 🔍 Research: Different jobs in community
  • ✏️ Write: "I want to be a..."
  • 🎨 Create: Role-play or poster
  • 🗣️ Speak: Interview a community helper

🐾 Weeks 5-7: Animals & Our Lives

Week 5-6: Wild Animals
  • 📖 Read: National Geographic Kids animal articles
  • 🔍 Research: Choose an animal, habitat, diet
  • ✏️ Write: Animal fact book
  • 🎨 Create: Animal diorama or drawing
  • 🗣️ Speak: "Amazing Animals" presentation
Week 7: Pets & Responsibility
  • 📖 Read: Pet care books
  • ✏️ Write: "How to care for a pet"
  • 🎨 Create: Pet care poster
  • 🗣️ Speak: Share about pet or desired pet

🌱 Weeks 8-10: Environment & Sustainability

Week 8-9: Nature & Our World
  • 📖 Read: "The Lorax" and environmental books
  • 🔍 Research: Climate, recycling, pollution
  • ✏️ Write: "How to protect Earth"
  • 🎨 Create: Recycling poster or nature art
  • 🗣️ Speak: "Green Tips" campaign
Week 10: Sustainable Living
  • 📖 Read: Zero waste stories
  • 🔍 Research: Ways to reduce, reuse, recycle
  • ✏️ Write: Family green pledge
  • 🎨 Create: Upcycling project
  • 🗣️ Present: Environmental action plan

💡 Weeks 11-14: Problem Solving & Action Design

Week 11-12: Identify Problems
  • 📖 Read: Problem-solving stories
  • 🔍 Research: Problems in community/school
  • ✏️ Write: Problem description & solution ideas
  • 🎨 Create: Problem-solution poster
Week 13-14: Design Your Solution
  • 📖 Read: Innovation stories
  • 🔍 Research: How others solved similar problems
  • ✏️ Write: Action plan
  • 🎨 Create: Prototype or model
  • 🗣️ Present: Proposal to family

🎤 Weeks 15-18: Portfolio & Final Presentation

Week 15-16: Portfolio Assembly
  • 📖 Review all work from Weeks 1-14
  • ✏️ Write: Introduction letter
  • 🎨 Organize: Best works, photos, videos
  • Create: Digital or physical portfolio
Week 17-18: Final Presentation
  • ✏️ Write: 2-3 minute English speech
  • 🎤 Practice: Rehearse presentation
  • Present: Family or friends audience
  • Celebrate: Certificate & reflection

🎯 Final Outputs

  • 📚 Portfolio: Collection of all work (writing, art, photos, videos)
  • 🗣️ English Presentation: 2-3 minute speech about learning journey
  • 🌟 Skills Gained: Research, writing, creativity, speaking, problem-solving

Created for Teresa | Grade 3-4 | Ages 8-9
